The Arts Initiative for Refugees (A.I.R.)

The Arts Initiative for Refugees (A.I.R.). A.I.R. strives to provide refugee youth with sessions, programs, and opportunities to help them achieve artistic and personal success through teaching and mentorship. This organization offers individualized and group mentoring programs to its students designed for their unique interests in the arts fields of their choice. The goal is for all artists within AIR to play & have fun, be immersed into different arts fields, build a community, create a safe & inclusive environment, and learn more about each other's backgrounds & cultures. Welcome to a community that demands & creates positive change! People of all identities and expressions are welcome and heard! Welcome to The Arts Initiative for Refugees!
